"Friends" is a 1990's sitcom that follows the personal and professional lives of 6 reckless 20-something year old adults in Manhattan. Ross, Rachel, Chandler, Monica, Joey, and Pheobe navigates the adversities of work, life, and love. Follow their journey through 10 season with approximately 15 - 25 episodes each.

After 17 years since the series finale of "Friends" aired, "Friends: The Reunion" was aired on 2021 under Max and Sky One. "Friends: The Reunion" is a 1 hour and 55 minutes long episode where the main casts revisit the sets and do re-enactments and table reads of iconic scenes, and share some never told before behind-the-scenes stories. They reminisce about the special relationships and bonds they have formed over the years.
