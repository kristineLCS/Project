About

"Friends" is a 1990's sitcom that follows the personal and professional lives of 6 reckless 20-something year old adults in Manhattan. Ross, Rachel, Chandler, Monica, Joey, and Pheobe navigates the adversities of work, life, and love. "Friends" is a 10 season sitcom with approximately 15 - 25 episodes each.
